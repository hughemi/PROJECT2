Australia, and the success they are having in reaching 80% double vaccination rates by the end of 2021

What continents and countries are struggling to get their population vaccinated?

Africa as a continent has a double vaccination rate of less than 4%, with some countries less than 1%, such as Cameroon, Chad, Ethiopia etc.

Due to most African countries being low-income and not having access to vaccines, could they hold the world back from finally eliminating COVID and opening all borders?

It is a real question that should be asked to all high-income, highly vaccinated countries. Australia is already sending millions of vaccines to the Pacific and Southeast Asia which is great, but with our extreme surplus of vaccines evidenced in previous graphs, should Australia look at assisting African countries as well?
